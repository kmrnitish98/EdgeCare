About Doctor

Dr. James Wilson is a highly skilled Neurologist with over 12 years of experience in treating complex neurological disorders. He completed his residency at Johns Hopkins Hospital and has been recognized for his research in stroke prevention and migraine management.

He believes in a patient-centric approach, ensuring that every individual receives a personalized treatment plan tailored to their specific lifestyle and needs.
