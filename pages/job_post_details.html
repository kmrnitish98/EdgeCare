About the Department

The Cardiology Department at EdgeCare VIP is recognized globally as a center of excellence. We leverage cutting-edge AI diagnostics and robotic surgery to provide unparalleled cardiac care. Our team is composed of industry leaders who are passionate about innovation and patient outcomes.

Job Description

We are seeking an experienced and compassionate Senior Cardiologist to join our world-class team. In this role, you will diagnose and treat patients with cardiovascular conditions, perform advanced cardiac procedures, and collaborate with a multidisciplinary team to provide the highest standard of patient care.

As a senior member of the department, you will also play a key role in mentoring junior doctors, participating in clinical research, and contributing to the continuous improvement of our cardiology services.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct comprehensive cardiovascular assessments and diagnose heart conditions using advanced imaging and testing.
  • Perform invasive and non-invasive cardiac procedures (e.g., catheterizations, echocardiograms).
  • Develop personalized treatment plans, including medication management and lifestyle counseling.
  • Collaborate with surgeons and other specialists for complex cases.
  • Lead clinical rounds and mentor residents/fellows.
  • Stay updated with the latest advancements in cardiology and telehealth integration.

Requirements

  • MD or DO degree from an accredited medical school.
  • Board Certification in Cardiology.
  • Valid medical license to practice in New York (or eligible to obtain).
  • 7+ years of clinical experience in a hospital or private practice setting.
  • Experience with telehealth platforms is a plus.
  • Strong communication skills and a patient-centric approach.
