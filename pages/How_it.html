Common Questions

Everything you need to know about the process.

Do I need insurance to use EdgeCare?
No, insurance is not mandatory. We offer transparent self-pay pricing for all consultations. However, if you do have insurance, you can add your details to see co-pay amounts.
Is my medical data secure?
Absolutely. We use military-grade AES-256 encryption for all data transmission and storage. Your records are HIPAA compliant and only accessible to you and your doctor.
Can I cancel or reschedule?
Yes, you can reschedule up to 2 hours before the appointment time without any penalty directly through the app dashboard.
How do video consultations work?
Simply log in to the EdgeCare app 5 minutes before your scheduled time. Click "Join Room" and you'll be connected instantly via a secure, high-definition video link.
