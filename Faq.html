Technical Support
How do I sign up for EdgeCare VIP?
Signing up is easy. Click the "Book Now" or "Log In" button at the top right, select "Register", and fill in your basic details. You'll gain instant access to our network.
Is my personal information safe?
Yes. We use end-to-end encryption and comply with all HIPAA regulations to ensure your medical and personal data remains completely private and secure.
Is EdgeCare available internationally?
Currently, our services are primarily available within the United States and Canada. However, our travel health advisory service is accessible globally for existing members.
How do I reset my password?
Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password?". Enter your registered email address, and we will send you a secure link to reset your credentials immediately.
Can I get a prescription online?
Absolutely. If the doctor deems it medically necessary, they can send a digital prescription directly to your preferred pharmacy immediately after your video consultation.
Are the doctors board-certified?
Yes, 100% of our specialists are board-certified and vetted through a rigorous background check process to ensure you receive top-tier care.
Can I request a specific doctor?
Yes! You can browse doctor profiles, read reviews, and select the specific specialist you wish to see. If they are unavailable, we will suggest equally qualified alternatives.
What if I need a specialist referral?
Our primary care physicians can issue digital referrals to specialists within the EdgeCare network or external providers, ensuring seamless continuity of care.
Do you accept insurance?
We accept most major insurance plans. You can upload your insurance card during booking to verify eligibility and view your co-pay amount before confirming.
What is the cancellation policy?
You can cancel or reschedule an appointment up to 2 hours before the scheduled time for a full refund. Late cancellations may incur a small fee.
How do I get a receipt for my visit?
Receipts and superbills are automatically generated after every consultation. You can download them anytime from the "Billing" section of your user dashboard.
Are there hidden fees?
Never. We believe in full transparency. The price you see during booking includes the consultation fee and administrative costs. Lab tests and medications are billed separately.
My video call isn't working. What do I do?
First, ensure you have a stable internet connection. If issues persist, try refreshing the page or switching browsers (Chrome is recommended). Our support team is also available 24/7.
Can I access my records from the app?
Yes, all your consultation notes, prescriptions, and lab results are automatically stored in your secure patient portal, accessible via the app or website at any time.
Does the app work on tablets?
Yes, the EdgeCare app is fully optimized for iPads and Android tablets, offering a larger screen experience perfect for video consultations.
How do I enable notifications?
Go to Profile > Settings > Notifications. We recommend keeping them on to receive timely appointment reminders and prescription updates.

Still have questions?

Our support team is just a click away.
